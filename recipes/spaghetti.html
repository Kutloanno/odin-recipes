Spaghetti Bolognese


Description

Spaghetti Bolognese is a classic Italian pasta dish made with a rich, meaty sauce. It is a hearty and satisfying meal perfect for lunch or dinner, combining the flavors of tender ground meat, savory tomato sauce, and aromatic herbs.

Ingredients

  • Spaghetti
  • Ground beef or pork
  • Tomato sauce
  • Onion (chopped)
  • Garlic (minced)
  • Olive oil
  • Carrot (finely diced)
  • Celery (finely diced)
  • Red wine (optional)
  • Salt and pepper
  • Fresh basil (for garnish)

Steps

  1. Cook the spaghetti according to package instructions.
  2. Sauté onion, garlic, carrot, and celery in olive oil until softened.
  3. Add the ground meat and cook until browned.
  4. Pour in tomato sauce and red wine (if using), then season with salt and pepper.
  5. Simmer the sauce for 20–30 minutes to develop flavors.
  6. Serve the sauce over the cooked spaghetti and garnish with fresh basil.
