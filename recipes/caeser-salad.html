Caesar Salad


Description

Caesar Salad is a popular salad featuring crisp romaine lettuce, creamy Caesar dressing, crunchy croutons, and grated Parmesan cheese. It is a versatile dish, often served as a starter, side dish, or light meal.

Ingredients

  • Romaine lettuce
  • Croutons
  • Parmesan cheese (grated)
  • Caesar dressing
  • Lemon juice
  • Olive oil
  • Garlic (minced)
  • Salt and pepper
  • Anchovies (optional)
  • Grilled chicken (optional, for protein)

Steps

  1. Wash and chop the romaine lettuce.
  2. Prepare the dressing by mixing Caesar dressing, lemon juice, olive oil, garlic, and optional anchovies.
  3. Toss the lettuce with the dressing until evenly coated.
  4. Add croutons and grated Parmesan cheese on top.
  5. Optional: Add grilled chicken slices for extra protein.
  6. Serve immediately and enjoy!
